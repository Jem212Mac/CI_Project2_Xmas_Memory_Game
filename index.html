How to Play

The aim of the game is to match all card pairs. Click on a card to flip it and display the card image. Click on a second card to reveal that card's image. If the second image matches the first image, the cards will remain faced up, and you can try to match another pair of cards. If the second image does not match the first image, both cards will flip over again and you will need to retry. Once all the cards are matched, you have won the game.
