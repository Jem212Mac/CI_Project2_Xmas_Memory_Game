Rules of the Game

The aim of the game is to match all pairs of card images. Click on a card to turn it over and display the card image. Click on a second card to display that card's image. If the second image matches the first image, the cards will remain turned facing up, and you can continue to try to match another pair of cards. If the second image does not match the first image, both cards will turn back over and you will need to try again. Once all the cards are matched, you have won the game. Your score will be dependent on the number of moves you made to win.
